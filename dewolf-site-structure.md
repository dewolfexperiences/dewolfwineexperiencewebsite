# Site Structure and Home Wireframe

## Recommended Pages
- Home / Podcast Landing: hero, listening platforms, featured episodes, newsletter, light intro to Mark.
- About Mark DeWolf: long-form credibility story (sommelier, writer, educator, events, Eat Drink Halifax, DeWolf Experiences, festivals).
- Episodes (optional): filterable list with players, show notes, tags such as Terroir, Food & Wine Pairing, Hospitality.
- Experiences & Events (optional): DeWolf Food & Wine Experiences, Halifax International Wine Festival, special dinners, tours.
- Contact / Newsletter (optional): simple form plus social links.

## Home / Landing Wireframe
1) Hero  
Logo top left (SVG or primary logo)  
H1: DeWolf Wine Experience  
H2: A wine and food culture podcast with sommelier Mark DeWolf  
Body: Tight intro about the podcast, Halifax base, two decades shaping food and wine via Eat Drink Halifax, DeWolf Food & Wine Experiences, Halifax International Wine Festival. Episodes explore people, places and traditions from Atlantic Canada and global regions.  
Primary CTA: Listen to the podcast  
Secondary CTA: View latest episodes  
Platform line: Listen on Apple Podcasts, Spotify, Google Podcasts, or wherever you get your podcasts.  
Platform badges: Apple, Spotify, Google, plus a More platforms link.  
Hero visual: SVG/logo + one strong image (Mark with a glass, vineyard, or Halifax waterfront).

2) Social Proof (optional but strong)  
Slim strip: "Mark DeWolf has led and written for" followed by Canadian Association of Professional Sommeliers, Association de la Sommellerie Internationale, Eat Drink Halifax, SaltWire, The Coast. Use logos if available, otherwise clean text.

3) Featured Episodes (above the fold or just below)  
Title: Start with these episodes  
Cards (3): episode title, 1–2 line description, tag (Terroir, Food & Wine Pairing, Hospitality), play button, View show notes link. Link internally to /episodes/slug.

4) What the Podcast Is About  
Title: What you will hear on DeWolf Wine Experience  
Intro: For curious wine drinkers, hospitality pros, and anyone who loves the intersection of wine, food and travel.  
Bullets:  
- Wine regions and terroir — conversations with winemakers, sommeliers and producers from Nova Scotia, Europe, North America and beyond, with focus on place and climate.  
- Food and wine pairing — practical ideas from chef-driven dinners, restaurant programs and Mark's events.  
- Hospitality and service culture — insight from a former national president of the Canadian Association of Professional Sommeliers, showing how storytelling and guest experience work in real life.  
- Travel and experiences — stories from DeWolf Food & Wine Experiences trips, Eat Drink Halifax collaborations, and festivals like the Halifax International Wine Festival.

5) Host Teaser  
Title: Hosted by Mark DeWolf  
Text: Halifax-based sommelier, writer, event creator and travel host. Certified since 2001; mentors young sommeliers; shapes national conversations on wine and hospitality. Creator of Eat Drink Halifax; owner of DeWolf Food & Wine Experiences; co-founder of the Halifax International Wine Festival; former national president of the Canadian Association of Professional Sommeliers; Content Manager and Editor for the Association de la Sommellerie Internationale. Philosophy: make wine welcoming, unpretentious and focused on what people actually enjoy.  
CTA: Learn more about Mark (link to /about).

6) Email Capture / Newsletter  
Title: Stay in the glass  
Copy: Get new episodes, event announcements and wine travel ideas delivered to your inbox.  
Fields: Email, optional first name.  
Button: Join the list.

7) Footer  
Small logo; links to Home, Episodes, About, Experiences, Contact.  
Short line: DeWolf Wine Experience is recorded in Halifax, Nova Scotia and available wherever you get your podcasts.

## About Page Structure
- H1: About Mark DeWolf  
- Intro paragraph: tight version of who he is (sommelier, writer, educator, events).  
- Section: A leading Canadian wine professional — certified since 2001; two decades plus; mentorship and national influence.  
- Section: Educator and industry leader — former National President of CAPS; sommelier instructor; supports Femmes du Vin and Tasting Climate Change; Content Manager and Editor for ASI Magazine.  
- Section: Writer and storyteller — roles at Occasions Magazine, NSLC, SaltWire, The Coast; focus on Atlantic Canadian restaurants, producers and culinary culture.  
- Section: Events, festivals and experiences — Eat Drink Halifax; DeWolf Food & Wine Experiences; Halifax International Wine Festival and Spirit Festival; trips, dinners, tastings.  
- Tie-back: Short paragraph on how this background feeds the DeWolf Wine Experience podcast.  
- CTA: Listen to the podcast.

## Asset Usage Notes
- SVG: Use as a subtle background pattern in the hero or as section dividers.  
- Logo: Header left and in the footer; in the hero, pair the logo lockup with the podcast name to signal brand immediately.  
- Imagery: Hero should feature one strong focal image (Mark, vineyard, or Halifax waterfront) for clarity and performance. About page: documentary-style shots (teaching, hosting, festivals). Experiences: event and travel photography. Avoid busy collages; prioritize a single clear hero image.
