### Body Copy
DeWolf Wine Experience is a wine and food culture podcast hosted by Halifax based sommelier, writer and event creator Mark DeWolf. For more than two decades he has helped shape how Canadians experience food and wine as the creator of Eat Drink Halifax, owner of DeWolf Food & Wine Experiences and co founder of the Halifax International Wine Festival.

Each episode uncorks the people, places and traditions behind the bottles we love. From Nova Scotia and Atlantic Canada to classic and emerging wine regions around the world, DeWolf Wine Experience blends storytelling, industry insight and hospitality driven education.

Mark has worked in wine and hospitality for more than 20 years as a certified sommelier, national leader and global educator. He is a former National President of the Canadian Association of Professional Sommeliers, a long time sommelier instructor and Content Manager for the Association de la Sommellerie Internationale, where he helps tell the stories of sommeliers around the world.

DeWolf Wine Experience brings that perspective to listeners who want more than tasting notes. You get context, culture and practical ideas you can actually use at the table.

## Host Block (Hero Sidebar or Below)
### H4
Hosted by Mark DeWolf

Mark DeWolf is a Halifax based sommelier, writer, event creator and travel host. He has:

- Been a certified sommelier since 2001
- Led the Canadian Association of Professional Sommeliers nationally
- Created Eat Drink Halifax and DeWolf Food & Wine Experiences
- Co founded the Halifax International Wine Festival and Halifax International Spirit Festival
- Written extensively on restaurants, producers and culinary culture across Atlantic Canada

His philosophy is simple: make wine welcoming, unpretentious and connected to the food and people you share it with.
